How It Works

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

Where do you get the lead information from? +
// Lead Sourcing

We pull business leads directly from Google Maps. We search for businesses in your target category and location — restaurants, salons, gyms, clinics, agencies, whatever your offer fits. From each listing we extract the business name, phone number, website URL, and category. That becomes your lead list. No shady databases. No bought lists. Real businesses that are publicly listed and actively operating.

How does the AI personalize each WhatsApp message? +
// AI Personalization

Once we have the lead list, our workflow visits each business's website automatically. The AI reads the site, identifies a specific pain point or weakness relevant to your offer, then writes a custom message for that exact business — not a copy-paste template. For example, if you sell booking software and a restaurant's site has no online booking, the AI will call that out directly in the message. It feels human because it is specific.

How does the message get sent without getting flagged as spam? +
// Delivery System

We use Evolution API connected to a real WhatsApp number. The workflow adds 45-second delays between each message and simulates typing indicators — so it behaves exactly like a human sending messages manually. Each contact is auto-marked as sent so there's zero risk of double-messaging anyone. This keeps your number healthy and your campaign running clean.

What do I need to provide to get started? +
// Getting Started

Just one sentence describing your offer and your target business type. For example: "I sell AI chatbots to restaurants in Lagos." That's enough. We handle lead extraction from Google Maps, website scraping, AI message writing, campaign configuration, and delivery. You approve the test batch of 5 messages before anything goes full scale — then you sit back and handle the replies.

What happens after messages are sent? +
// After Campaign

You receive a Google Sheet with every business we contacted — name, number, website, and message sent. You manage replies directly from your WhatsApp. We deliver a full campaign report with delivery confirmation. If you're on the Growth or Agency tier, we also give you a performance summary with insights on what's working.

Can I run this system myself after you set it up? +
// Ownership

Yes — that's exactly what the White Label / Custom tier is for. We configure the entire system under your brand, hand over full access, and train you or your team to run it independently. You can then offer this as a service to your own clients at whatever price you choose. You own the system. We just build and hand it over.
